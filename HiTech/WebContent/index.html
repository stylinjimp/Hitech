The Facts
Research Estimates:
  • 26 million people suffer from asthma.
  • Children lose 14 million school days a year due to asthma & allergies.
  • Air inside of your home is at least 10 times more polluted than outdoor air.
  • Indoor air pollution is one of the top 5 health concerns of the American Lung Association.

♦ Since 1990, the EPA has consistently ranked indoor air pollution as one of the top 5 environmental risks to public health.

♦ Indoor air quality directly affects our health, comfort, productivity, and quality of life.
  • Sensitive populations like asthma and allergy sufferers, children, the elderly, pregnant women and those with respiratory diseases are especially at risk.

♦ According to the American Lung Association, 26 million Americans suffer from asthma with 9 million of these people under 18 years old.
  • Asthma is the most prevalent chronic disease among children.

♦ Airborne particle allergens such as dust, pollen and smoke can trigger or worsen asthma, allergies or other respiratory conditions.

Indoor Air Quality - Research Results
♦ AirAdvice tested almost 50,000 homes over three years, looking at the following indoor air quality issues:
  • Particle allergens (airborne particulate matter like dust, pollen, etc.)
  • Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs)
  • Carbon Dioxide
  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Carbon Monoxide

♦ Almost 97% of the tested homes had IAQ problems.

♦ Over 90% of the homes showed particle levels higher than recommended.

♦ An HVAC manufacturer did an IAQ survey of consumers to determine the public's in IAQ and in home evaluations.

♦ 58% of the consumers surveyed were either "very concerned" or "concerned" about the quality of air in their home.

♦ 76% of the respondents said they would be either "very interested" or "interested" in an IAQ evaluation of their home.

♦ Of those that were interested in having an evaluation, the most common reasons they were interested were:
  • Allergy or asthma concerns.
  • Desire to breathe cleaner air
  • Protection for kids or family
